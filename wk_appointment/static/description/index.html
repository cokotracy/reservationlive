Manage the Charges for the Appointments

You need to simply create a service type product to create an appointment.

Appointment Charges can be managed from various forms in the module depending upon the preference of the user.

The Appointee allows you to manage the charges according to individual appointee irrespective of the group.

If appointment charges are assigned to the individual then the priority is given to individual appointment charges over group charges.

If the individual charges are left zero, the group charges are displayed in the form.

Key Pointers

  • The customers can book the appointments from the Odoo website.
  • The appointees can be seen from the website while booking the appointments.

Now, send SMS notifications for Appointments to Customers

Appointment SMS Notification Management Module is specifically developed to keep your customers up to date about the progress of their appointment requests. Once configured, it sends automatic SMS notifications to your customers. It also sends reminders to notify about an upcoming appointment. Get the module and reach out to your customers on their mobile phones.

Key Pointers

  • Send SMS notifications about successful appointment request generated and change in status of appointment request.
  • Send reminders about an upcoming appointment. Select the days/hours before the date of appointment to send the reminders.
  • View the record of every notification and reminder sent to each customers in the Odoo.
